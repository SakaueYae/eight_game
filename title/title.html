    You'll go into the next shop! Then compare it with "Shop 0" and...
    • turn back if you feel that something is different🧐!
    • if you don't feel anything different, keep going!
    ⚠️ Be aware that if you make the wrong decision to "turn back or go forward", you'll have to start over from "Shop 0"! ⚠️

TIPS

ここに注目して間違いを探してみてね🙌🏼
It's easier to spot mistakes if you look here.

  • 黒板の画鋲・大きさ/
  • チラシの文字（大文字か小文字か）
  • 窓・扉の大きさ，形
  • ランプの位置

基本操作/Basic Operations

"０番店"と比べて違いが発見できたら左キーを押す．そうでなければ，そのまま右キーを押す
If you find a difference compared to "shop 0", press the left key. Otherwise, press the right key.
